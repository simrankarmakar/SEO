How Nykaa Ranks for Beauty & Skincare Keywords

Target: "buy makeup online" & beauty category terms

1. Introduction

Target keyword

Primary: "buy makeup online", "best skincare products", "nykaa". Nykaa targets both brand and category keywords.

Search intent

Mix of transactional (buy makeup online) and informational (best skincare routine). Content and category pages are built to match both.

Why this brand ranks

Nykaa owns the beauty e-commerce space in India with strong domain authority, category-depth content, and a clean site structure that supports both product and blog rankings.

2. Keyword Analysis

  • Primary keywordbuy makeup online
  • Secondarybest lipstick, skincare routine, k-beauty
  • Long-tailbest moisturizer for oily skin, lipstick under 500
  • Search volume (est.)High (10K–100K+)
  • DifficultyHigh (competition from Amazon, Myntra)

Nykaa clusters keywords by category (makeup, skincare, haircare) and intent (buy vs. learn), which supports internal linking and topical authority.

3. On-Page SEO Analysis

Title tag optimization

Category pages use keyword-rich titles, e.g. "Makeup – Buy Cosmetics Online | Nykaa". Brand + category + CTA.

Meta description

Compelling, under 160 characters, with keywords and urgency (offers, free delivery).

URL structure

Short, readable: /makeup, /skincare, /brands. Product URLs include category and product name.

Heading structure

Single H1 per page, logical H2/H3 for filters, subcategories, and content blocks.

Internal linking

Strong hub-and-spoke: category → subcategory → product. Blog links to relevant category and product pages.

4. Off-Page SEO

  • BacklinksStrong; partnerships, PR, influencer content
  • Domain authority (est.)High (70+)
  • Referring domainsDiverse (media, beauty blogs, affiliates)

Nykaa earns links through product launches, beauty guides, and brand campaigns, which reinforces topical authority for beauty keywords.

5. Technical SEO

  • Page speedOptimized; critical CSS, lazy load for images
  • Mobile friendlinessMobile-first; app-like experience on mobile
  • Schema markupProduct, Organization, BreadcrumbList
  • Site architectureFlat enough for important categories; clear hierarchy

6. Why They Rank

Summary: Key ranking factors

  • Topical authority in beauty & skincare with deep category and content coverage.
  • Strong backlink profile and brand visibility.
  • On-page alignment with search intent (transactional + informational).
  • Technical foundation: fast, mobile-friendly, with schema.
  • Internal linking that distributes authority to key category and product pages.
