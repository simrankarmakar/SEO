Competitor Analysis

Comparing Nykaa, Amazon India, and Flipkart: content depth, backlink profile, and content gaps in e-commerce and marketplace SEO.

Overview

We compare three major players in Indian e-commerce and marketplace SEO: Nykaa (beauty), Amazon India (general marketplace), and Flipkart (marketplace with strong sale events). This helps you see how different strategies play out in content, links, and technical execution.

Content Depth

Nykaa

Deep content in beauty: category pages, brand hubs, and blog content (beauty tips, routines). Strong topical authority in makeup, skincare, haircare. Content supports both transactional (buy) and informational (how-to) intent.

Amazon India

Extreme depth at scale: millions of product pages, A+ content, reviews, Q&A. Category pages are thin compared to product depth. Authority comes from breadth and user-generated content, not long-form guides.

Flipkart

Category and sale-focused content. Sale landing pages are content-rich (deals, categories, countdown). Less emphasis on evergreen blog content; more on category and event pages. Good depth in key categories (electronics, fashion).

Takeaway: Nykaa leads in editorial depth in a niche; Amazon leads in product and UGC depth; Flipkart leads in event and category landing pages.

Backlink Profile

  • Amazon IndiaExceptional; one of the most linked domains globally. Affiliates, reviews, news.
  • FlipkartStrong; media coverage, sale events, brand campaigns. High authority.
  • NykaaStrong in beauty; PR, influencers, beauty blogs. Niche authority.

Domain authority (estimated): Amazon > Flipkart ≈ Nykaa in absolute terms, but Nykaa’s links are highly relevant to beauty, which helps for beauty-specific keywords.

Content Gaps

Opportunities where one player is weak and another is strong:

  • Nykaa vs Amazon/Flipkart: Nykaa owns beauty how-to and routine content. Amazon and Flipkart have limited editorial content in beauty; opportunity for them to add guides and routines.
  • Amazon/Flipkart vs Nykaa: Amazon and Flipkart dominate generic "buy online" and broad category terms. Nykaa focuses on beauty; less opportunity for Nykaa on non-beauty categories.
  • Flipkart: Sale-event content is a strength; evergreen category guides (e.g. "how to choose a laptop") are a gap compared to Amazon’s product detail and reviews.

For your own site: use these gaps to find topics and keywords where you can out-depth or out-intent competitors.

Summary

Quick comparison

  • Content depth: Nykaa (beauty editorial), Amazon (product + UGC), Flipkart (sale + category).
  • Backlinks: Amazon > Flipkart ≈ Nykaa in scale; Nykaa strong in relevance for beauty.
  • Content gaps: More editorial in beauty (Nykaa’s strength); more evergreen guides for categories (Flipkart’s gap); Amazon strong everywhere but not deep in niche editorial.
